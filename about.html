About Cycling hub

Cycling Hub is a platform for cyclist and cities to improve cycling infrastructure. Cycling Hub is a platform for cyclist and cities to improve cycling infrastructure. Cyclists can add, like and comment on reports on the map to warn other cyclists and to show the city which are the most critical areas to fix. Cities receive this feedback directly and based on this they can prioritize and optimize infrastructure work without need of reading redundant feedback. This project is made in collaboration with city of Espoo and is part of Design project course of Aalto university.
